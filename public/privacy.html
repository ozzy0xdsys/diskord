Privacy policy

Privacy

Diskord is built around ephemeral sessions. Session content is not stored long‑term and is deleted when the session creator ends the session.

End-to-end encryption

Chat messages and shared files are encrypted in your browser using E2EE before being sent. The server only relays encrypted data between participants.

What we do not log

  • We do not log IP addresses.
  • We do not log user agents.

Accounts

Your account stores a username and a password hash. Optional profile pictures are stored as an image blob.
