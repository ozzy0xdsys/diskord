Privacy Policy

Passwords are stored as SHA-256 hashes with a per-user salt. Diskord never stores your plaintext password.

Device info (including IP addresses and User-Agent strings) is never logged under any circumstances by the Diskord application.

Messages are end-to-end encrypted: the server stores ciphertext and cannot read message contents.

Note: if you run Diskord behind a reverse proxy (e.g., Nginx/Cloudflare), that proxy may keep its own access logs unless you disable them.
